usage: dvc remove [-h] [-q | -v] [--outs] targets [targets ...]

Remove stages or .dvc files, unprotect their outputs, and erase .gitignore entries.
Documentation: <https://man.dvc.org/remove>

positional arguments:
  targets        DVC-files to remove.

optional arguments:
  -h, --help     show this help message and exit
  -q, --quiet    Be quiet.
  -v, --verbose  Be verbose.
  --outs         Remove outputs as well.
