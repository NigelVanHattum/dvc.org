usage: dvc unprotect [-h] [-q | -v] targets [targets ...]

Unprotect tracked files or directories (when hardlinks or symlinks have been enabled with `dvc config cache.type`)
Documentation: <https://man.dvc.org/unprotect>

positional arguments:
  targets        Data files/directories to unprotect.

optional arguments:
  -h, --help     show this help message and exit
  -q, --quiet    Be quiet.
  -v, --verbose  Be verbose.
